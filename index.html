A simple page about a not so simple person who enjoys knitting, drinking and learning to code

Knitter, fan of Craft Beer, lover of brandy Old Fashioneds & Bloody Marys, paper crafter & Aspiring code writer


I am a happily married woman who is lucky enough to love life!



I have spent most of my life knitting, and have become a yarn snob who makes mostly yummy squishy socks..



My husband and I enjoy visiting brew pubs anywhere we go. We have even been know to plan our vacations around good beer =)



Loving beer is an obvious lead in for our Bloody Mary adventures! You would be amazed at how many different ways alcohol and tomato can be combined!



We also love to take our Travel Trailer camping as often as possible, preferably to County camp grounds...
Walking to supper clubs to have a good meal and some brandy Old Fashioneds is one of the best parts of camping!


There are many weekends during the summer that are spent camping in friends yards, it lets us stay at the camp fires much later that way!
